These pages document the classes that comprise the core of Multigraph. If you're starting from scratch trying to make sense of this, read through the classes in the following order, which in general gives simpler classes before more complicated ones:
  1. {@link org.multigraph.DataValue}
  2. {@link org.multigraph.NumberValue}
  3. {@link org.multigraph.DatetimeValue}
  4. {@link org.multigraph.DataMeasure}
  5. {@link org.multigraph.NumberMeasure}
  6. {@link org.multigraph.DatetimeMeasure}
  7. {@link org.multigraph.DataVariable}
  8. {@link org.multigraph.Data}
